A cookie is a tiny bit of information saved on your device by websites you visit. It helps remember your preferences, track your actions, and create personalized experiences. Cookies store details like login information, site preferences, and shopping cart contents. They're often used to enhance website functionality, improve user experience, and show targeted ads. However, privacy concerns have led to more awareness and regulations about how cookies are used.
